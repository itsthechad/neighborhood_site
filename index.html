Welcome to Windwood North

Windwood North is a beautiful, quiet neighborhood situated in Dunwoody, Georgia. Here, mature trees line the roads, friendly residents wave as they pass, and families gather at the local park.

Though quiet and secluded, the neighborhood has easy access to I-285, meaning you're never far from where you want to be.

Welcome Home

Windwood North is a non-HOA community with approximately 120 single-family homes. We are located in Dunwoody and are part of DeKalb County.

Trees, Trees, Trees

Gorgeous, mature trees abound in Windwood North, providing shade, privacy, and beauty.

Love, Love

Family Friendly

Windwood Hollow Park just had a major makeover in the form of an amazing new playground. Best of all, it's just down the street.

Neighborhood Park

Windwood Hollow Park is a Dunwoody public park, though we tend to think of it as our own neighborhood park since it's nestled in at the entrance of the neighborhood.
